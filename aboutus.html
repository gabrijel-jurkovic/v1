Our story

We know full well that buying tickets can be a hassle and like You, we too have often times found ourselves in the unpleasant situation of going to an event only to find out that the venue has just been filled up. That is why we designed this app with the sole purpose of making your ticket buying experience and event organizing as easy as possible. Launched in 2021 by five collage students, MeetUp is an online event booking app that lets you check out events and plan your own with only a few clicks. Browse hundreds of events, from concerts and parties to book fairs and seminars. If you somehow don't find what you are looking for, you can just as easily make your own event.
